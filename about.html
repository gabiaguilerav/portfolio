About me

Born and raised in Mexico City, I’ve have always been an enthusiast of graphics and bring my ideas to life. I studied design and visual communication at the FAD, which is small school part of the UNAM located in the south part of Mexico city. I moved to Germany after graduating from University and started a trip to find myself and figure out what I would like to do for a living.

In the city of Köln I got to connect with another creative people, such as artists, musicians and web designers. This people gave me indeed a lot of perspective about the market job and inspired me to get into web development. The journey of learning coding hasn’t been easy but it’s been very gratifying.

I’m currently living in the Netherlands, near the nice city of Maastricht. My hobbies are mostly related to artistic/cultural activities, but I also love doing yoga, rollerskating and of course going out to drink a good cup of matcha.
